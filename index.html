RaceVision AI

Harnessing artificial intelligence to transform horse‑racing analysis

Overview

The thrill of the track lies not only in who wins but in how the race unfolds – the break from the gates, the battles for position and the surging finish. For punters these moments are everything, yet the clues they offer are buried in hours of video and rows of numbers. RaceVision AI is built for punters: by applying cutting‑edge computer vision and automated editing we transform raw race footage into dynamic, personalised form guides. Within minutes you see where a horse settled, when it made its move and how it responded to pressure – all the information you need to punt with confidence.

Our flagship product identifies horses using their racing colours and physical characteristics, tracks their positions throughout the race and compiles bespoke highlight reels that reveal tactical patterns and hidden trends. It reduces analysis time by 90 percent and surfaces insights that even expert reviewers miss. The goal is simple: give punters a decisive edge in one of the largest wagering markets on earth.

NextPlay AI is the venture studio behind RaceVision AI. We harness the trust and passion inherent in sport and combine it with artificial intelligence to build a portfolio of ventures that change the way fans engage, train, learn and play.

This site provides a comprehensive overview of our venture. You can explore the details of our idea, the market opportunity, the technology powering our product, the commercial model and partnerships, our financials and risk mitigation, and the team driving the vision.
