About NextPlay AI

NextPlay AI is a venture studio that combines the trust and passion of the sporting world with the transformative power of artificial intelligence. With billions of sports fans worldwide and rapid advances in computing, we see an unprecedented opportunity to build ventures that improve lives while delivering strong financial returns.

Our platform focuses on creating innovative solutions that leverage athletes’ credibility and the massive reach of sport. We recognise that supporters follow players and teams with an unparalleled level of trust; by harnessing this goodwill and combining it with AI, we can develop products and services that resonate, inspire and drive change.

Portfolio strategy

NextPlay AI takes a portfolio approach to venture building. We develop a mix of micro solutions, medium‑sized ventures and major ventures across six verticals: education & human performance, performance & training, funding & financial infrastructure, community & connection, professional pathways, and climate & lifestyle. Each venture is designed to address a real‑world challenge through the lens of sport, from improving athletic training to driving climate action.

Our first two ventures include an elite sports academy initiative and a climate‑action platform that mobilises fans to reduce carbon footprints through sporting heroes. RaceVision AI, the horse‑racing intelligence platform described throughout this site, is our third venture. By diversifying across multiple projects, we manage risk while maximising impact.

The NextPlay advantage

We believe our advantage lies in three core capabilities:

  • Access to sport federations & athletes: Exclusive partnerships with leagues and top athletes give us unparalleled reach and authenticity. These relationships enable rapid adoption of our products and high levels of user trust.
  • Shared AI infrastructure: We invest once in a robust AI backbone – including data pipelines, computer‑vision models and cloud infrastructure – and deploy it across multiple ventures. This shared platform accelerates development and reduces costs.
  • Risk‑managed portfolio: By operating several ventures simultaneously, we balance early‑stage experimentation with more mature revenue streams. Profitable products support emerging ideas, creating a sustainable pipeline of innovation.

Our roadmap

Over the next 12 months we plan to expand our micro solutions, roll out our first partnership‑driven venture and launch the beta of RaceVision AI. At the same time, we will secure further agreements with sports federations and prepare for the next round of funding to support additional ventures. Our ambition is to demonstrate the power of sport‑powered AI to investors, partners and fans alike.
